Why Durably

Know the true cost

Understand how depreciation, desired lifespan and maintenance affect the real cost per month of every asset you own.

Stay on top of warranties

Store warranty documents and receive alerts before deadlines or maintenance dates pass you by.

Celebrate milestones

Durability achievements keep you motivated and highlight the items that outperform expectations.

Loans under control

Link loans to each item, review amortizations and forecast monthly payments with confidence.

Batch power tools

Update multiple items at once, filter by status or category, and export your data whenever you want—the information is yours.

Widgets & reminders

Access key metrics from your Home Screen and receive proactive reminders so nothing slips through.

Take control of every euro you invest

Durably lets you plan purchases, track their performance and decide when it’s time to replace or sell. The result is a healthier, more mindful relationship with the things you own.

  • Record items with images, desired lifespan and value forecasts.
  • Log recurring events, repairs and maintenance costs.
  • Compare categories to see which areas of your life need attention.
